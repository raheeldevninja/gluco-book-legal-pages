Delete Account - Gluco Book

Last updated: June 27, 2026

Gluco Book allows users to delete their account and associated data.

How to delete your account in the app

  1. Open the Gluco Book app.
  2. Sign in to your account if you are not already signed in.
  3. Open the navigation drawer by tapping the menu icon in the top-left corner.
  4. Tap Delete Account.
  5. Review the confirmation message.
  6. Tap Delete Account.
  7. Enter your account password to verify your identity.

What data will be deleted?

  • Your account information.
  • Your glucose/sugar readings.
  • Your medicine and dosage records.
  • Your walking and medicine taken status records.
  • Your reading history and report-related data.

What data may be retained?

We do not intentionally retain your personal health data after account deletion. Some data may remain temporarily in secure backups or logs where required for security, legal, or technical reasons.
