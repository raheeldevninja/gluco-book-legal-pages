Privacy Policy for Gluco Book

Last updated: June 27, 2026

Gluco Book is a diabetes sugar reading note-taking app developed by Raheel Khan. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Gluco Book mobile application.

Medical Disclaimer: Gluco Book is designed for personal record keeping only. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, or emergency medical services. Always consult a qualified doctor or healthcare professional for medical decisions.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Gluco Book, we may collect and store the following information:

  • Name and email address for account creation and login.
  • Diabetes sugar/glucose readings entered by you.
  • Date and time of readings.
  • Medicine intake information, including medicine name and dosage if provided by you.
  • Walking status and medicine taken status.
  • Reading history, weekly average readings, latest reading, and reading statistics.
  • Generated reports and PDF export data created from your readings.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information only to provide the core features of the app, including:

  • Creating and managing your account.
  • Saving and displaying your sugar readings.
  • Showing your latest reading, reading history, weekly averages, and graphs.
  • Allowing you to add, view, and delete readings.
  • Allowing you to record medicine intake and walking status.
  • Generating PDF reports for your personal use or for sharing with your doctor.
  • Improving app reliability and user experience.

3. Firebase Services

Gluco Book uses Firebase services provided by Google, including Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore. Firebase Authentication is used to create and manage user accounts. Cloud Firestore is used to store your app data securely in the cloud.

Your data may be processed by Firebase according to Google's Firebase and Google Cloud policies.

4. Health Data

Glucose readings, medicine information, dosage information, and related diabetes records are considered sensitive health-related data. We collect and store this information only because you enter it into the app and because it is necessary for the app's functionality.

We do not sell your health data. We do not use your health data for advertising purposes.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal or health-related data to third parties.

Your data may only be shared in the following limited situations:

  • With Firebase/Google services required to operate the app.
  • If required by law, regulation, legal process, or government request.
  • If you personally choose to export or share a PDF report with someone else, such as a doctor or family member.

6. PDF Reports and Exports

Gluco Book allows you to generate PDF reports from your readings. These reports are created for your personal use. If you share the PDF file with another person or app, you are responsible for that sharing.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. Data is stored using Firebase services, and communication with Firebase is encrypted in transit. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. Data Retention

Your data is kept as long as your account exists or as long as needed to provide app functionality. You may delete readings from within the app where available.

9. Account and Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at: [YOUR_EMAIL]

When requesting deletion, please contact us from the same email address used for your Gluco Book account. We may need to verify your identity before deleting account data.

10. Children's Privacy

Gluco Book is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

11. Medical Responsibility

Gluco Book does not replace professional medical advice. The app does not make treatment decisions and should not be used as the only source for managing diabetes. Always consult your doctor before making changes to medicine, diet, exercise, or treatment.
